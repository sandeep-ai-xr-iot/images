# Privacy Policy for GlassPin

Last updated: June 27, 2026

GlassPin is an application that uses the Pinterest API to help authorized users read Pinterest board information and create Pins.

## Information We Access

When you authorize GlassPin through Pinterest, the app may access the following Pinterest account information based on the permissions you approve:

- Read access to your Pinterest boards.
- Write access to create Pins on your behalf.

GlassPin only requests the Pinterest API scopes needed for these features, including `boards:read` and `pins:write`.

## How We Use Information

We use Pinterest API access only to:

- Display or identify Pinterest boards available to your authorized account.
- Create Pins on boards selected or authorized by you.
- Complete actions that you initiate within the app.

We do not sell your Pinterest data.

## Data Storage

GlassPin does not intentionally store your Pinterest account data for resale or advertising purposes. If access tokens, board identifiers, Pin identifiers, or related technical data are stored, they are used only to operate the app features you authorized.

## Data Sharing

We do not share your Pinterest data with third parties except when necessary to operate the app, comply with legal obligations, protect rights and safety, or as required by Pinterest API functionality.

## User Control

You can revoke GlassPin's access to your Pinterest account at any time through your Pinterest account settings.

If you want data associated with your use of GlassPin deleted, contact us using the contact information below.

## Security

We use reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect information handled by the app. However, no internet-based service can guarantee complete security.

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Updates will be posted on this page with a revised "Last updated" date.
